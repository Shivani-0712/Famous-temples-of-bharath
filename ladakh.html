Kali Mata Mandir

Location: Within Spituk Monastery, approx. 8 km from Leh, Ladakh

Deity: Mahakala (a fierce Buddhist guardian deity)

Architecture: Ancient black stone statues in upper monastery chambers

Kali Mata Mandir Details

Unique Feature: Idol of Mahakala unveiled only once a year

Festival: Celebrates the Gustor Festival with mask dances and rituals

Significance: Revered by Tibetan Buddhists; fusion of Hindu and Buddhist elements

Access: Requires climbing to upper levels of Spituk Monastery
