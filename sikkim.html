Kirateshwar Mahadev Temple

Location: Near Legship, West Sikkim, on the banks of the Rangit River

Deity: Lord Shiva (Kirateshwar form)

Significance: Believed to be where Lord Shiva tested Arjuna

Kirateshwar Mahadev Temple Details

Pilgrimage Site: A sacred site visited by locals and tourists alike

Festivals: Bala Chaturdashi (Nov–Dec) and Maha Shivaratri (Feb–Mar)

Legend: Lord Shiva, in the form of a hunter (Kirata), tested Arjuna's devotion here

Atmosphere: Located in a peaceful riverside setting with a highly spiritual ambiance

Legship Thakurbari Temple

Location: Legship town, West Sikkim

Deity: Lord Vishnu (Thakur)

Importance: One of the oldest Hindu temples in Sikkim

Legship Thakurbari Temple Details

Architecture: Simple structure with traditional Hindu design elements

Community Role: Central religious center for the local Hindu community

Festivals: Janmashtami, Ram Navami, and other Vaishnavite celebrations
