Durgiana Temple – Amritsar

Location: Amritsar, near the Golden Temple

Dedicated to: Goddess Durga (also Lakshmi and Narayan)

Architecture: Resembles the Golden Temple, surrounded by a holy tank

Durgiana Temple Details

Also Known As: Lakshmi Narayan Temple or Silver Temple (due to silver doors)

Built In: 1908 by Harsai Mal Kapoor

Spiritual Importance: Major Hindu pilgrimage site in Punjab

Festivals Celebrated: Navratri, Dussehra, Diwali

Mata Mansa Devi Temple – Panchkula

Location: Panchkula (near Chandigarh), Haryana

Dedicated to: Goddess Mansa Devi (form of Shakti)

Situated at: Foothills of the Shivalik range

Mata Mansa Devi Temple Details

Built In: 1815–1816 by Maharaja Gopal Singh of Mani Majra

Spiritual Belief: Fulfills wishes (“Mansa” means wish/desire)

Major Festival: Navratri Mela – held twice a year with massive crowds

Temple Complex: Expansive area with scenic views
