Siddhivinayak Temple

Location: Prabhadevi, Mumbai, Maharashtra

Deity: Lord Ganesha

Architecture: Modern structure with gold-plated inner sanctum

Siddhivinayak Temple Details

Established: In 1801 by Laxman Vithu and Deubai Patil

Significance: Believed to fulfill wishes of devotees

Unique Feature: Ganesha idol has a right-turned trunk and is made of black stone with a tilted trunk

Popular Among: Celebrities, politicians, and common devotees alike

Festivals: Ganesh Chaturthi is celebrated with great grandeur

Darshan Tip: Tuesdays are especially crowded due to the special aarti

Trimbakeshwar Temple

Location: Trimbak town, near Nashik, Maharashtra

Deity: Lord Shiva (as Trimbakeshwar – “Lord of the Three Eyes”)

Architecture: Built with black stone in classic Hemadpanthi style

Trimbakeshwar Temple Details

Significance: One of the 12 Jyotirlingas in India; birthplace of the Godavari River

Unique Feature: Linga has three faces representing Brahma, Vishnu, and Shiva

Kumbh Mela: Held every 12 years here along with Nashik

Rituals: Known for Narayan Nagbali, Kalsarpa Dosha, and Pitru Dosh pujas
