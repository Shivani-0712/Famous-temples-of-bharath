Dimapur Kalibari Temple

Location: Dimapur, Nagaland

Dedicated to: Goddess Kali

Established: Built in 1956

Dimapur Kalibari Temple Details

Architecture: Traditional Hindu temple design with intricate carvings

Cultural Importance: Major center for Bengali Hindu community in Dimapur

Festivals: Celebrates Kali Puja, Durga Puja, and Diwali with great enthusiasm

Community Role: Acts as a spiritual and cultural hub for organizing religious events

Hanuman Mandir (Dimapur)

Location: Dimapur, near Railway Station area

Dedicated to: Lord Hanuman

Spiritual Significance: Symbol of strength, protection, and devotion to Lord Rama

Hanuman Mandir Details

Visitors: Popular among locals and travelers, especially on Tuesdays and Saturdays

Festivals: Celebrates Hanuman Jayanti and Ram Navami

Ambience: Known for its peaceful, spiritual environment
