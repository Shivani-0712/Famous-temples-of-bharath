Bhuteshwar Mahadev Temple

Location: Mathura, approx. 2 km from Mathura Junction Railway Station

Deity: Lord Shiva, revered as Bhuteshwar Mahadev

Spiritual Significance: One of the 51 Shakti Peethas

Bhuteshwar Mahadev Temple Details

Spiritual Significance: Believed to be where a ringlet of Mata Sati fell

Unique Feature: A rare Lord Shiva temple in Krishna's city

Historical Note: Contains Patal Devi Cave associated with King Kansa

Visiting Hours: 5:00 AM – 1:00 PM and 4:30 PM – 10:30 PM

Sheetla Mata Mandir

Location: Mata Pokhara, Mau, approx. 2 km from Mau Railway Station

Deity: Goddess Sheetla, protector against diseases

Cultural Role: Considered the Kuldevi (family deity) by locals

Sheetla Mata Mandir Details

Festivals: Navratri and Diwali celebrated with devotion

Importance: Worshipped for protection against diseases like smallpox

Accessibility: Easily reachable via local transport from railway and bus stations
